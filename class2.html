Class 2 Title: Developing Intelligent Content (ENGL 4814)

Professor Name: Carlos Evia

Description:

Carlos Evia is a professor in the School of Communication, Associate Dean for Transdisciplinary Initiatives, and Chief Technology Officer at Virginia Tech. His research focus is technical and professional communication and content strategy. He has written multiple books and articles, notably, Creating Intelligent Content with Lightweight DITA.
