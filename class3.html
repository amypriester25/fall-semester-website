Class 3: Public Relations Campaigns (PR 4304)

Course Description:

Professor Name: Chelsea Woods

Description:

Chelsea Woods is a professor in the School of Communication at Virginia Tech. She teaches public relations courses including Issues Management and Public Relations Campaigns. Her research focus is crisis communication and issue and reputation management. She has published several journal articles and her research has appeared in the Journal of Public Relations Research. She received her bachelor's degree in communication studies from Campbell University, her master's from the University of North Carolina at Charlotte, and her Ph.D. in communication from the University of Kentucky.
