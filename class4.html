Class 4: Creating User Documentation (ENGL 3814)

Course Description

Creating User Documentation explores the different avenues of user documentation. Every week we learn about a different type of user documentation and work on creating one ourselves. This class is particularly helpful for technical communication students.

Professor: Sidouane Patcha-Lum

Sidouane Patcha-Lum teaches technical writing in the Department of English at Virginia Tech. She instructs multiple professional and technical writing courses every semester including Writing and Digital Media and Creating User Documentation. Her research focus is user experience and technical writing. She completed her undergrad in journalism and mass communication at the University of Buea in Cameroon, West Africa, received her master's in rhetoric and technical communication from Michigan Technological University, and her Ph.D. in rhetoric and composition from the University of Texas at El Paso.
